Lion

The lion is a species in the family Felidae and a subspecies of the larger Panthera pardus.

Tiger

Tigers are the largest cats, native to the Americas and Asia.

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest living land animals, with adults reaching heights of up to 9 feet (2.7 meters).
