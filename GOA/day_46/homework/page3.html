Elephant

Elephants are the largest living land animals and the only species of the family Elephantidae. They are native to Africa and Asia.

Kangaroo

Kangaroos are marsupials native to Australia and New Guinea. They are known for their hopping and strong hind legs.

Whale

Whales are a group of marine mammals, characterized by their large size and streamlined bodies. They are known for their intelligence and complex social structures.
